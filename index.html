Welcome to the Society Survey and Election Management Portal.

Purpose of the survey and polling

The purpose of the Survey and Polling System is to provide a centralized, efficient, and transparent platform for residential society members to participate in surveys and elections. The system enables society residents to share their opinions, provide feedback on community-related matters, and vote for representatives in a secure and organized manner.

Election Announcement

The Society Representative Election for the upcoming term will be conducted online through this portal.
